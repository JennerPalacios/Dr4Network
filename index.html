Dr4con1um's Survival Server
( Vanilla Minecraft v1.15.2 )

QUICK INFO
  • When you die you will drop a chest. You will have 10 minutes to pickup the items; if you don't make it in time, the chest will disappear. Click the chest in order to pickup the items; The items will be equiped (if any) and will return to the same order you had them, prior to dying.
  • Player backpacks available. You cannot drop the backpack; you can move backpack around to a different slot by opening your inventory; if you put it where an item is, the item will go inside the backpack, you can also place items in your backpack by dropping them into the slot where the backpack is. Rightclick the backpack to open it, if in the inventory, you can also open the backpack by rightclick it if you have it in the hotbar.
  • More craftable armors available, with special effects:
    • Titan Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Item Ability: Health

      Increases max health by 2.

      Full set bonus: Resistance

      Gives Resistance I for ten seconds after killing a mob.

    • Emerald Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Item Ability: Emerald Blood

      Every 50 emerald ores mined will increase max health by 2.

      Max: 10 health per armor piece.

    • Speedster Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Item Ability: Speed

      Increases max health by 20%.

      Full set bonus: Retreat

      Gives Speed II for five seconds when taking damage.

    • Experience Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Full set bonus: Experience

      Recieve double experience from mining and killing mobs.

    • Miner Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Full set bonus: Haste

      Gives Haste II for five seconds after mining a block.

    • Nether Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Item Ability: Bane of the Nether

      Gives +10% damage against Magma Cubes, Zombie Pigmen, Ghasts, Blazes, and Wither Skeletons.

      Full set bonus: Nether King

      Gives Fire Resistance while in the Nether.
      Increases max health by 20 while in the Nether.
      Deal +100% damage while in the Nether.

    • End Armor (show effects | crafting)
      Item Ability: Bane of the End

      Gives +10% damage against Endermen, Endermites, and Ender Dragons.

      Full set bonus: End King

      Teleport with Sneack+RightClick, while holding an item(sword).

  • Pet-wolves are able to level up; they gains more health and strength as a player does through killing mobs.
  • If you find a spawner in a dungeon, and you want to take it home in order to create your own XP-farm, you can mine it with a Silk Touch Pickaxe.
  • There are shops available near spawn, feel free to stop-by and purchase some items, gear, and weapons. You can also sell your excess items like Cobblestone, etc.

Grief Prevention
  • Prevents other players from:
    - Destroying personal stuff (builds)
    - Stealing stuff (food, items in chest)
    - Killing animals (farm animals and pets)
    - Disables PvP in claimed areas (public and private)
  • It also enables collaboration for building together, such as a city, a room inside your home, etc.
  • To start claiming land you will need to get a Golden Shovel.
  • You can also place a chest in order to claim a small box around the chest; chest will not be accessible by anyone, only its owner.
  • You can equip a regular Stick in order to see the borders of a claim.
